About Little Tokyo

Little Tokyo Sushi Bar & Grill has been serving RTP residents with high quality of food and service for thirty years.

 

Little Tokyo Sushi Bar & Grill is the best choice for diners who want to experience authentic Japanese food at affordable prices. The owner Mr. Tanaka, Ahn, who worked in the center of Japan as a sous chef and moved to North Carolina to recreate international cuisine. The depth of his cooking knowledge and his customer oriented philosophy make Little Tokyo Sushi Bar & Grill a popular place in the RTP area. Fresh ingredients, high quality of food, and friendly service are the critical factors in the success of Little Tokyo Sushi Bar & Grill.

 

Little Tokyo Sushi Bar & Grill is a full service restaurant with take out available. We serve lunch Monday through Saturday and dinner Monday to Sunday. The menu includes special house rolls with delightful sauces, fresh sashimi and sushi, and traditional Japanese cuisine made for those who go for their food cooked including diverse noodles, tempuras, teriyaki and hibachi entrees.

 

Come experience the relaxing and comfortable dining ambience for yourself.
