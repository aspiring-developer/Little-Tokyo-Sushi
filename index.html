Welcome to Little Tokyo!


Little Tokyo Sushi Bar and Grill has been serving RTP residence with high quality food for thirty years. We heartily appreciate our customers who provide us great opportunity to serve Japanese taste in the town.


Little Tokyo is a full service restaurant with take out service. Fresh ingredients, high quality food, and friendly service are the critical factors in the success of Little Tokyo. Our experienced chefs and servers are always eager to provide great customer service in a healthy environment.


We invite you to experience and enjoy our relaxing and comfortable dining ambience.

Our menu includes special house rolls with delightfulsauces, fresh sashimi and sushi, and traditional Japanese cuisine made for those who want their food cooked including diverse noodles, tempuras, teriyaki and hibachi entrees.

The owner of Little Tokyo, Mr.Ahn Tanaka, is making sushi.

We proudly support smoke free dining! Glad to show this Certificate of Appreciation.
